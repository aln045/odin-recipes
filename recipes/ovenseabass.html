Oven Sea Bass

Description

This oven-baked whole sea bass is a simple yet flavorful meal that's perfect for any night of the week. With just a handful of ingredients, it delivers fresh, delicious results with minimal effort.

Preparation takes only 10 minutes, and the fish cooks quickly in the oven, making it an easy choice for a fast dinner. The straightforward recipe lets the natural taste of the sea bass shine through.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole sea bass
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse the sea bass under cold water and pat it dry.
  3. Place the sea bass on a baking sheet.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Stuff the cavity with lemon slices and garlic.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
