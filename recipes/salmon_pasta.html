Creamy Salmon Pasta

Description

This creamy salmon pasta combines tender flakes of salmon with a rich, velvety sauce and fresh spinach for an elegant yet easy meal. It's a great recipe when you want something comforting without spending hours in the kitchen.

The smooth cream sauce coats every strand of pasta, while the salmon and spinach create a delicious balance of flavor and freshness. It's a restaurant-quality dish made with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine or linguine
  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and cook the salmon for 4-5 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the salmon from the skillet and flake it into bite-sized pieces.
  5. In the same skillet, sauté the garlic for 1 minute.
  6. Add the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Stir in the spinach and cook for 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  8. Add the cooked pasta and salmon, tossing to coat in the sauce.
  9. Season with parsley, salt, and pepper to taste.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
